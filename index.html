About me

Professionally I am a solution specialist who focuses primarily on Microsoft technologies. My current area of focus is Azure Infrastructure as a service and also on premises private cloud solutions.


I also have a lot of experience working with System Centre Configuration Manager, Application packaging, Operating System Deployment and also a fair amount of scripting in various languages (PowerShell, VBScript, JavaScript, Python, Ruby, etc).


On top of the script skills above I also have software development skills primarily in C#. These skills include proper usage of Patterns and also use of development tools like source control, continuous integration tools etc.

Despite being focused on Microsoft technologies professionally I am also proficient in using both OS X and Linux. I have the attitude that most operating systems have similar functions; it is just a matter of learning the process of getting what you want.


Outside of work I am also passionate about game development and have been building my skills by competing in Ludum Dare events that come up every quarter. Ludum Dare requires participants to create a video game from scratch in 48 hours.


Another new passion of mine at the moment is DevOps. I love the idea of a workflow where it is easy to test changes before putting them into production and also automating as much of the process as possible.
